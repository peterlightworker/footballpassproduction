Who We Are

MEET GRANT PHILIPS

Match Director & Founder

At the helm of Football Pass Productions FZE LLC is Grant Philips - an Executive Producer, Match Director, and multi-Emmy Award winner with over 3,000 live matchday credits across a distinguished 30-year career in sports television production.

From FIFA World Cups to Copa America finals, from the Saudi Pro League to the UEFA Champions League, we've delivered world-class coverage for the biggest competitions, broadcasters, rights holders, and federations across the globe.

Career Highlights

  • FIFA World Cup 2026 USA (incl. Semi Final)
  • FIFA World Cup 2022 Qatar (14 matches incl. Semi & 3rd/4th)
  • Copa America 2024 (Opening Ceremony + Final)
  • FIFA Club World Cup 2023 & 2025
  • CONCACAF Gold Cup (Multiple editions + Finals)
  • UEFA Champions League & Europa League (2000 - Present)
  • Saudi Pro League (2023 - Present)
  • Premier League (1999 - Present)
